* If maximized:
** M-Up: Nothing, M-Down: Minimize
** M-Left: Half screen
*** M-Left: Full Screen
*** M-Right: Right half
** M-Down: original position

* if not maximized:
** M-Left: half screen
*** M-Left: original position
*** M-Right: half screen
** M-Up: full screen

- M-f: f11
- M-j/k: ctrl+alt+left/right
